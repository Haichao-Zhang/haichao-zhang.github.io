### Welcome

This will be my new journey on logging and sharing some interesting results I got during my daily research.


This will be my new journey of logging and sharing some interesting results I encounter during my daily research.

Over time, I’ve found that many ideas—small observations, partial results, or even failed experiments—tend to get lost if they are not written down. This blog is an attempt to capture those moments: quick insights, intermediate findings, and evolving thoughts in machine learning, robotics, and embodied AI.

The goal is not to present only polished outcomes, but to document the process itself. Some posts may be rough, exploratory, or even speculative. That’s intentional. Often, the most valuable ideas emerge from iteration rather than final results.

I also hope this serves as a lightweight way to:
- track progress over time
- revisit and refine ideas
- share insights that might be useful to others

Let’s see where this goes.
